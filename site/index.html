Three Days · Three Stories

From bondage to the far shore.

Kids hear the story together, then break into age-appropriate groups for drawing, coloring, worksheets, puzzles, and discussion — age 3 through 6th grade.

  1. I Day One

    Slavery

    God's people in Egypt cry out. He calls Moses from the burning bush, and the long work of deliverance begins.

    Exodus 3 · 6 · 7

  2. II Day Two

    The Plagues

    Ten signs over Egypt. The river turns red, the sky goes dark, and a hard heart meets the steady hand of God.

    Exodus 7 — 11

  3. III Day Three

    Passover & Red Sea

    Blood on the doorpost. Waters held back. A people walks out free — pointing forward to a greater Lamb.

    Exodus 12 — 14
